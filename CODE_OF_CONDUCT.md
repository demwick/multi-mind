# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or inflammatory language
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact: **talhademirell@outlook.com**

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
